ClubGoGo

This app is developed for the freshman, who want to make new friends and know little about Stony Brook clubs. In the app, users can click the SBU map to check out the details of clubs in the building user clicked. Also, user can search a club by keywords that they might be interested, such as boxing club, chess club and Chinese culture club. The goal of the app is to help students new to the school be more engaged and enjoy their college time by joining club activities.
